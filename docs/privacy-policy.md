# SkinShelf Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-12

SkinShelf stores the account, skin profile, shelf products, routine data, assistant chat history, and skin tracking logs needed to run the app.

Skin photos are processed for analysis and are not stored by default. The app stores the generated skin analysis result and related form answers, not the raw image file.

Users can delete their account from the Profile screen. Account deletion removes the user account, skin profile, products, assistant messages, and skin tracking logs from the backend database.

SkinShelf does not provide medical diagnosis. Assistant and analysis responses are informational and should not replace professional medical advice.
