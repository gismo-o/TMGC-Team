# SkinShelf Terms of Use

Last updated: 2026-07-12

SkinShelf helps users organize skincare products, routines, and skin tracking notes.

The app does not diagnose, treat, or prevent medical conditions. If a user experiences severe burning, swelling, open wounds, eye-area reactions, infection symptoms, or persistent concerns, they should contact a dermatologist or health professional.

Users are responsible for checking product labels, patch testing when appropriate, and stopping product use if unexpected irritation occurs.

SkinShelf may provide personalized suggestions based on profile answers, shelf products, and user-entered notes. These suggestions are informational and may be incomplete or inaccurate.
